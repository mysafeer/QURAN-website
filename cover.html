First Revelation

The first revelation came to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in the cave of Hira during Ramadan. The first word revealed was "Iqra" meaning "Read" or "Recite".

02

Preservation

The Quran is the only religious text that has been preserved in its original language without any changes for over 1400 years.

03

Scientific Miracles

The Quran contains numerous scientific facts that were unknown at the time of revelation, including descriptions of embryonic development and water cycle.

04

Mathematical Miracle

The word "day" (yawm) is mentioned 365 times, "month" (shahr) 12 times, and "sea" (bahr) 32 times while "land" (barr) 13 times - the ratio matches Earth's surface.

05

Chapters and Verses

The Quran contains 114 chapters (Surahs) and 6,236 verses (Ayahs). The longest chapter is Al-Baqarah with 286 verses.

06

Recitation Reward

Reciting one letter of the Quran brings 10 rewards. The Prophet (PBUH) said: "Whoever recites a letter from the Book of Allah will be credited with a good deed."
